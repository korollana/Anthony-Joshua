Anthony Joshua Net Worth: Anthony Joshua is a professional boxer from the United Kingdom with a huge net worth of $80 million
who has won the unified world heavyweight title twice. He is one of the world’s highest-paid athletes having made $50-60 million
a year or more at his height. From 2015 to 2016, he held the British and Commonwealth heavyweight titles at the regional level.
He was born in Watford, Hertfordshire, on October 15, 1989, to Yeta and Robert Joshua. His mother is of Nigerian descent,
and his father is of Nigerian and Irish descent. Ben Ileyemi, his cousin, is also a professional boxer. He attended Mayflower School
in Ikenne as a boarding school student during his early years in Nigeria. He returned to the UK halfway through to attend
Kings Langley Secondary School after his parents divorced when he was 12 years old. He was a standout in football and sports,
setting a new school record in the Year Nine 100 meter dash with a time of 11.6 seconds.
